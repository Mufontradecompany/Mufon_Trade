Cumaru

Cumaru wood is obtained from trees of the species Dipteryx odorata. In Brazil, it is very common in the states of the North, Northeast and Central-West regions, as well as in other countries such as Bolivia, Peru, Colombia and Venezuela. ​​

Garaperia

Garapeira wood is valued for its long durability, guaranteeing an attractive appearance for many years, especially when used and processed properly. Its ease of handling is attributed to the presence of silica. However, it is essential to note that working with this wood requires appropriate tools to obtain the best results. Because it is a heavy wood with high durability and medium to high mechanical resistance, garapeira wood is used in specific applications.

Angelim Stone

Angelim stonewood comes from the tree with the scientific name Hymenolobium petraeum Ducke. This species is native to several regions of Brazil, including Amazonas, Acre, Amapá, Mato Grosso, Pará, and Rondônia. It can also be found in other countries such as Guyana, French Guiana, and Suriname. ​ Stability, durability, and versatility make angelim stone an excellent choice of wood for decks and floors! Although it is a somewhat heavy wood, it is easy to work with and screw. For this reason, angelim stone is widely used in the manufacture of doors and windows.

Brazil Refined Icumsa 45 /100 / 150+

Brazilian ICUMSA 45 is a premium, highly refined sparkling white cane sugar, characterized by a color rating under 45 ICUMSA units (RBU). It is the highest quality sugar available, with 99.8% minimum polarization, making it ideal for direct human consumption, food industries, and beverages due to its high purity.

Key Characteristics & Specifications Color: Sparkling White. Origin: Brazil (typically shipped from Santos or Paranaguá ports). Polarization: Minimum. Ash Content: Maximum. Moisture: Maximum. Solubility: Dry and Free Flowing. Radiation: Certified free from Cesium or Iodine.

Packaging

  • 25 kg and 50 kg polypropylene (PP) bags
  • Jumbo bags (1-1.5 MT)

YELLOW CORN

Yellow corn for export is primarily traded as a high-energy commodity used for animal feed (poultry, swine, cattle), industrial applications (ethanol, starch), and food processing (grits, meal). The most commonly exported type is Yellow Dent Corn, which is characterized by its yellow color (due to high beta-carotene/vitamin A content), high starch content, and hard kernel structure. .

Yellow corn Packaging

Typical Export Specifications (Grade 2/3) Variety: Yellow Dent Corn (Non-GMO usually specified for European markets). Moisture Content: 13% – 14.5% maximum (higher moisture increases spoilage risk during long-term shipping). Admixture/Foreign Matter: 1% – 2.5% maximum. Broken Kernels: 2% – 5% maximum. Damaged Kernels (Total): 3% – 5% maximum (includes heat-damaged, molded, or rotten). Protein: 9% minimum. Aflatoxin: Below 20 PPB (parts per billion) max. Bulk Density/Hectoliter: Minimum 68-72 kg/hl (high density indicates sound grain). Free from: Live pests, insects, chemicals, and fermentation

Palm Kernel Fatty Acid Distilled (PKFAD)

Palm Kernel Fatty Acid Distillate or commonly known as PKFAD is produced during the refining process of the Palm Kernel based oils. PKFAD is widely used in the oleo chemicals industries, for making soap and detergent and shampoos.

Palm Fatty Acid Distilled (PFAD)

The PFAD is a by-product of crude palm oil (CPO) refining. PFAD is inedible and low price palm oil product making it attractive as a raw material for soap industry and feedstock for biofuel. PFAD consists of these degraded fats that are undesired for food production and need to be removed during the palm oil refining process before the oil meets the food industry’s quality standards.

SURFACTANTS:

LABSA 96% & 90%

Linear Alkyl Benzene Sulphonic Acid is a largest volume synthetic surfactant because of its relatively low cost and good performance. LAB Sulphonic Acid is an anionic surfactant widely used in formulation of all ranges of Domestic Detergents Powder ,Cake & Dish wash cleaners. Due to its high active matter , miscibility with water and low salt content , it is also used in formulation of Industrial & Household liquid cleaners as well as in numerous industrial applications like as a coupling agent and as an emulsifier for agricultural herbicides and in emulsion polymerization.

SLS & SLES 70%

Sodium laureth sulfate, an accepted contraction of sodium lauryl ether sulfate, is an anionic detergent and surfactant found in many personal care products. SLES is an inexpensive and very effective foaming agent.

Methyl Ester Sulfonate (MES)

Methyl ester sulfonates (MES) are anionic surfactants that can be made by sulfonation of saturated fatty acid methyl esters, derived from natural fats and oils. It is used in manufacturing of Laundry Detergent powders & detergent cakes and act as a surfactant giving cleansing property. It has high detergency, water hardness tolerance property, and biodegradable ability. It is available in powder form.
